## Verdict Scale
- **CRITICAL** — do not merge; breaks existing functionality or introduces a serious bug
- **HIGH**     — significant issue; needs fixing before merge
- **MEDIUM**   — addressable with a small change; can merge after fix
- **LOW**      — minor; can merge, open a follow-up ticket

## Final Output
End your summary with one of: **APPROVE** / **REQUEST CHANGES** / **BLOCK**
and a single sentence explaining why.
